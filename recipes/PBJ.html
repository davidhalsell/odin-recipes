Peanut Butter & Jelly

Description

A simple sandwich made with peanut butter and your favorite flavor of jelly.

Ingredients

  • Peanut Butter
  • Jelly (grape or strawberry recommended)
  • Two slices of bread

Steps

  1. Place slices of bread on plate
  2. Spread peanut butter thickly on one slice
  3. Spread jelly thickly on other slice
  4. Put the two slices together and enjoy!

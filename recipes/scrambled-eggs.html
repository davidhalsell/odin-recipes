Scrambled Eggs

Description

Scrambled eggs made with sour cream for maximum fluffiness, topped with black pepper for a bit of zing

Ingredients

  • Four Eggs
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • Sour Cream

Steps

  1. Gently crack eggs and pour yolks into whisking bowl
  2. Add a dallop of sour cream to yolk and whisk until smooth
  3. Pour into pan on stove, with heat on medium
  4. Slowly stir eggs to make sure they don't burn as they cook
  5. After about 15 minutes, eggs should look fluffy and ready to serve
  6. Add salt and pepper to taste
